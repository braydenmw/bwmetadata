
# Stakeholder Requirements: MainCanvas Component Failure Impact

## Stakeholder Analysis

### Primary Stakeholders

#### 1. Business Analysts
**Role**: Primary users of the Unified Control Matrix for strategic analysis
**Impact**: 
- Cannot create strategic roadmaps
- Unable to generate market intelligence reports
- Blocked from partnership opportunity analysis
- Inability to complete daily work assignments

**Requirements**:
- Immediate restoration of strategic analysis capabilities
- Access to all existing data and configurations
- Seamless continuation of ongoing analysis projects

#### 2. Partnership Managers  
**Role**: Responsible for developing and managing strategic partnerships
**Impact**:
- Cannot access partnership development tools
- Unable to evaluate potential partners
- Blocked from creating partnership proposals
- Delayed partner communications and negotiations

**Requirements**:
- Restoration of partnership profiling capabilities
- Access to partner compatibility analysis tools
- Ability to generate partnership documents

#### 3. Executive Leadership
**Role**: Decision-makers relying on strategic intelligence
**Impact**:
- Cannot access critical market intelligence reports
- Delayed strategic decision-making
- Inability to review partnership recommendations
- Reduced visibility into business development pipeline

**Requirements**:
- Immediate access to strategic dashboards
- Ability to review and approve partnership strategies
- Visibility into business development metrics

### Secondary Stakeholders

#### 4. Clients
**Role**: Recipients of strategic analysis and partnership services
**Impact**:
- Delayed delivery of strategic reports
- Postponed partnership strategy sessions
- Potential loss of competitive advantage
- Reduced confidence in service reliability

**Requirements**:
- Timely delivery of committed deliverables
- Communication about resolution timeline
- Assurance of data integrity and quality

#### 5. Internal Teams (Sales, Marketing, Operations)
**Role**: Collaborators in strategic initiatives
**Impact**:
- Delayed cross-functional strategic planning
- Inability to access market intelligence for planning
- Postponed launch of partnership initiatives
- Reduced coordination on strategic projects

**Requirements**:
- Restoration of collaborative planning capabilities
- Access to shared strategic documents
- Ability to coordinate on partnership initiatives

## Risk Assessment

### High-Risk Factors
1. **Client Relationship Damage**: Extended downtime may damage client trust
2. **Competitive Disadvantage**: Delayed strategic responses to market opportunities
3. **Revenue Loss**: Inability to deliver billable strategic services
4. **Employee Productivity**: Staff unable to perform core job functions

### Mitigation Strategies
1. **Transparent Communication**: Regular updates to manage expectations
2. **Alternative Solutions**: Manual workarounds for critical deliverables
3. **Priority Focus**: Restore most critical functions first
4. **Compensation Planning**: Client service credits for significant delays

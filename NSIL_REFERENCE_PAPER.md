## ━━━ GLOSSARY ━━━

**Driver** — Factor that increases a score.

**Pressure point** — Factor that reduces a score or increases risk.

**Gating factor** — Something that blocks activation until resolved.

**P10/P50/P90** — Optimistic / Median / Conservative scenario bounds.

**Bisociation** — Creative connection between two unrelated frames of reference (Koestler).

**Structural twin** — Region with similar economic/demographic/institutional profile (not aspirational peer).

**Simulacrum** — Loss of authentic competitive identity; generic, interchangeable positioning (Baudrillard).

**DAG Scheduler** — Directed Acyclic Graph that manages formula dependencies, enabling parallel execution of independent formulas and caching of intermediate results.

**Hard Gate** — An ethical or compliance check that blocks a recommendation entirely (not just flags it) when thresholds are not met.

**NSIL** — Nexus Strategic Intelligence Layer — the reasoning engine that wraps around all scoring systems and coordinates the full analytical pipeline.
